About Show-Me Psilocybin

Show-Me Psilocybin is a grassroots effort to bring guided, therapeutic psilocybin treatment to the people of Missouri.

We believe psilocybin is not a recreational substance — it’s a therapeutic compound that, under the guidance of trained professionals, can support healing from trauma, depression, addiction, PTSD, and end-of-life anxiety. Our vision is focused solely on clinical use in structured, counselor-supported environments.

Why Missouri? Why Now?

Our state has long struggled with access to quality mental health care. From veterans coping with PTSD to families battling generational trauma, Missourians deserve more treatment options — especially those backed by science.

Psilocybin therapy is already making a difference in places like Oregon, Colorado, and in clinical research institutions across the country. We believe Missouri deserves that same opportunity — not later, but now.

Who We Are

This movement began with a single Missourian — someone inspired not by profit or politics, but by a deep sense of duty to improve lives. Motivated by research, grounded in ethics, and fueled by compassion, that spark became Show-Me Psilocybin.

We’re a small but growing team of Missourians — students, veterans, therapists, parents, professionals — united by the belief that healing should be available to those who need it, not criminalized.

Join Us

We are in the early stages of building Missouri’s first therapeutic psilocybin initiative. If you believe in expanding access to safe, evidence-based mental health care, we invite you to stay connected, get involved, and help shape what comes next.
