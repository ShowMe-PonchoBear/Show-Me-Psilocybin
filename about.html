About Show-Me Psilocybin

Show-Me Psilocybin is Missouri’s grassroots push for the safe, legal, and therapeutic use of psilocybin mushrooms. Our goal is to decriminalize and eventually legalize psilocybin for clinical use, research, and personal healing — starting right here in the heart of America.

This movement was founded by someone who deeply understands the healing potential of psilocybin. After years of hands-on experience, careful cultivation, and a sincere respect for the mushroom, it became clear: Missouri deserves access to this medicine.

We believe in smart policy, safe practices, and science-backed reform. We believe the people of Missouri are ready.
